ASWWU Townathlon (TNT) 2016

When: Sunday, October 2, 2016 at 1 p.m. Pre-run on-site registration (tickets sales, bib assignments, waivers) starts at 12.00 p.m., the run itself begins at 1 p.m. after a thorough group warm-up.
What: Associated Students of Walla Walla University Townathlon ( ASWWU TNT) is a fun obstacle run through the picturesque Bennington Lake recreational area.
Yes, thank you, but where exactly: Please see the run maps here.
Who: the run is open to all fun-loving participants ages 14 and above.
How: you can pay the TNT fee here or on the day of the run during registration. We appreciate early sign ups as it helps immensely with planning. You will need to fill out and sign the hard copy of the liability waiver during pre-run registration to participate in the run. Please read the liability waiver here. All participants will be required to present valid photo identification. And don't forget that minors (under 18) will need parental/legal guardian consent to run.
How Much: $20 - for ages 14 to 17 and for students (valid student ID required), $40 - for working adults 18 and up. Prices are per one participant.
Posters: right here
Additional Questions: please visit our TNT Q&A page here.
Even More Questions: please call (555) 555-5555 or email aswwu.globalservice@wallawalla.edu

Townathlon 2016 proceeds will go to ASWWU Global Service mission project. It is a local run with a global impact!

Please join us at the Townathlon to promote fun and healthy lifestyle in the Walla Walla Valley and to support the mission of ASWWU Global Service!
